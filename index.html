  1. Upload an image (AI or photo).
  2. Extract the original palette, tweak if needed.
  3. Build a restricted palette (enable/disable inks).
  4. Manual mix: replace a color with tiles of 2–3 inks (set block/pixel size & pattern).
  5. Map to palette (choose preview scale) and review.
  6. Export PNG/SVG and vectorize in Illustrator.

About

Cup Mapper helps convert complex art into limited-ink imagery designed for reliable vectorization in Adobe Illustrator and cup printing workflows.
